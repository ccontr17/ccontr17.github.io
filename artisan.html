INTRO

Jhené Aiko Efuru Chilombo (born March 16, 1988), most commonly known simply as Jhené Aiko, is an American alternative RnB singer from Los Angeles, CA.

EARLY LIFE

Jhene Aiko is the youngest daughter born to her father Karamo Chilombo and mother Christina Yamamoto. She grew up in the Rodeo and LaBrea neighborhoods of Los Angeles. She was homeschooled for much of her life.

MUSIC CAREER


Her music career started with contributing vocals and appearing in music videos for RnB group B2K at the age of 12. At 15, she had signed with record label, Epic, and was set to release her own solo album until she asked to be released due to creative differences with the label manager. She took a short hiatus from creating music in 2003 to finish her education and released her first mixtape, Sailing Soul(s), in March 2011, which is the album that set her onto the mainstream RnB music scene. She has collabed with big names such as Drake, Childish Gambino, 2 Chainz, Ty Dolla $ign, and Kendrick Lamar. She is currently dating rapper Big Sean and have a stagename together called TWENTY88.
