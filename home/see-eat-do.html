SEE

Three of my favorite places to see in San Francisco.
Obviously you can’t think about San Francisco without thinking of the Golden Gate Bridge! Ain’t it such a beauty.
Second would be Ina Coolbrith Park for a great view of the San Francisco skyline.
And lastly, Dolores Park in the Mission District.
It’s my go-to spot to just chill and catch up with some friends.

EAT

THE best part about San Francisco is all the amazing FOOD.
Starting off with my favorite local bakery, Vive la Tarte.
They are known for their beautiful croissant creations and their modern interior.
Super Duper Burgers, my FAVORITE burger place of all time. Get the garlic fries, trust me.
And my most recent favorite place, Tartine Manufactory.
You may have heard of Tartine as the most famous bakery in all of San Francisco.
It is an amazing place to have brunch.

DO

San Francisco has no shortage of amazing museums and galleries.
With over 8 floors to explore, The San Francisco Museum of Modern Art (SFMOMA)
is the place to go to see art in all its forms; sculpture, painting, installations, graphic design, and photography.
The Museum of Ice Cream (MOIC) is a pop-up museum where you can explore the different rooms and
take pics for your instagram (and eat lots of ice cream of course!).
Lastly, the Chandran Gallery is my newest discovery of SF galleries to visit.
They are a contemporary art gallery that rotate what’s on display every few month.
On display right now is “Intertwine”, an installation by Rebecca Louise Law, all real flowers! Plus it’s free to visit!
